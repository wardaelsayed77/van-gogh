The Potato Eaters,

The Potato Eaters is an oil painting by Dutch artist Vincent van Gogh in April 1885 in Nuenen, the Netherlands. The painting is now in the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam. A preliminary oil painting of her is also in the Kröller-Müller Museum in Otrillo. A lithograph of the portrait, preserved in the Van Gogh Collection at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City.In December 1988, a crude copy of The Potato Eaters was stolen, and on July 14, 1989, the painting was recovered.
